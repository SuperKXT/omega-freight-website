Why Choose Us?

The biggest concern for any trucker is where to find loads, being that’s the very foundation of what their business is built on, we understand that concern.
That being said, we at Omega Freight & Logistics seek to provide that peace of mind by working with the best freight brokers in the industry to help new owner-operators build and scale their business as they desire. We can book many loads as you can legally and safely handle.
Our goal is to provide you unbeatable service at an unbeatable price. Other dispatchers are charging upwards of $350/weekly. We will provide you our full service for $250/weekly!!! Let’s take a look at the breakdown…
The average trucker drives about 2500 miles per week. With that being said let’s say the average rate per mile is $1.90. Per day the average miles driven, based on what we have above is 500 mile per day. The average load you may carry could be about 750 miles roughly.
750mile X $1.90/mile=$1425
Most dispatchers will charge a percentage for each load they book for the driver. In this case the dispatcher who is charging a modest 6.5% for his/her services would earn $92.63 from this driver for this one load.
Based on the mileage we determined the average driver to drive per week, this would equate to about 3 loads if the driver desires to be home every weekend, because, let’s be honest, some drivers do want to be home on weekends.
$92.63 X 3 loads = $277.89/week which is $1111.56/monthly..
Keep in mind this is using averages so the loads could pay more and have more mileage, which means this cost could be higher. Based on our modest service fee of $250 per week you would simply pay
$250 X 4 weeks = $1000/monthly
No matter how long your trip is we only collect $250 per week and you get to keep the rest.

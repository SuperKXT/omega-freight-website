Who We Are

Omega Freight & Logistics LLC is a veteran owned small business with over 20 years of logistics experience.
I served in the United States Navy, where I was the leading logistics specialists aboard several ships and shore commands.
I was honorably discharged in 2016, with the idea of starting a business with my daughter. Our father-daughter team has a passion for logistics and ensuring the satisfaction of our clients, customers, and partners.
We truly understand the importance of timely service, and of having a reliable team to help execute great service. This is our focus here at Omega Freight & Logistics, not only to provide you with great customer service, but to ensure that we provide the support needed for you to do the same for your customers.
Please allow us to serve you and help grow your business with our unique business structure that is designed to keep your trucks loaded and money in your pocket
